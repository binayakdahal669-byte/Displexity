1. **tui_enhanced.disll** (TUI Rendering)
   - Terminal initialization and ANSI color support

   - Chunk management and frustum culling
   - HUD and overlay rendering

   - 160x40 character resolution at 200+ FPS

2. **math_3d.disll** (3D Mathematics)
   - Vector operations (add, subtract, scale, dot, cross, normalize)
   - Matrix operations (identity, translation, rotation, scale)
   - 3D projection (perspective, orthographic)
   - Camera frustum culling
   - FPS calculation and performance metrics

3. **raytracer.disll** (Real-Time Raytracing)
   - DDA (Digital Differential Analyzer) voxel grid traversal
   - Ray-AABB intersection for voxel hit detection
   - Surface normal calculation
   - Lighting calculations (diffuse, ambient, specular)
   - Shadow ray tracing
   - Reflection and recursive bounces (up to 3)
   - Ambient occlusion
   - Performance optimization techniques

4. **audio_system.disll** (Sound System)
   - Audio device initialization (48kHz, stereo)
   - Procedural sound synthesis (sine, square, sawtooth, noise)
   - 3D spatial audio positioning
   - Distance-based attenuation
   - Block interaction sounds:
     * Footsteps on different surfaces
     * Block breaking/placing sounds
     * Jump/fall sounds
   - Ambient music system
   - Reverb and echo effects
   - Voice/text-to-speech support

5. **texture_mapper.disll** (Texture System)
   - Texture atlas management (16x16 tiles)
   - Block texture assignment (10+ block types)
   - Animated textures (water, lava with 8 frames)
   - UV coordinate calculation and rotation
   - Texture compression and mipmaps
   - Biome color tinting
   - Procedural texture generation
   - Normal mapping support
